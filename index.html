About Us

iMOB Racing thrives to be the biggest team in the racing industry. We are a group of racecar modders and enthusiasts primarily focused on time attack racing, but we also engage in 4x4, rally, drifting, off-road, street bikes, dirt bikes, and crosskarts. We are 100% built, not bought, modifying and customizing any vehicle. Our co-founders, Anthony Yorba and Tony Langston, lead the team with technical expertise and artistic vision. Our top driver, Chris Statler, embodies our racing spirit, while our youngest member, Daniel Bertholf, excels in drifting. We aim to build our brand with exciting race footage, informative repair videos, and behind-the-scenes content. We plan to offer giveaways and forums for discussions, event dates, and buy/sell opportunities.
